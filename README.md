## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a **Power BI dashboard** focused on analyzing heart disease patient data to uncover key insights related to survival, age distribution, and the impact of medical conditions such as diabetes and cancer.

The goal is to transform raw healthcare data into **actionable insights** for better understanding patient outcomes.

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives

- Analyze survival vs fatality trends
- Understand age-wise survival distribution
- Evaluate the impact of diabetes and cancer on survival
- Build an interactive and visually engaging dashboard

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Key Metrics (KPIs)
- **Total Survivors**
- **Survival Rate (%)**
- **Average Age of Survivors**
- **Total Fatalities**

### 🔹 Visual Insights
- Survival Distribution by Age Group
- Diabetes Trend & Ranking by Age Group
- Survival Count by Cancer Presence
- Avg Serum Sodium & Mortality Analysis

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

- Highest survival is observed in the **51–70 age group**
- Patients with **diabetes show lower survival trends**
- **Cancer presence reduces survival count significantly**
- Survival rate is influenced by age and comorbid conditions
